libigl is a simple C++ geometry processing library. We have a wide functionality including construction of sparse discrete differential geometry operators and finite-elements matrices such as the cotangent Laplacian and diagonalized mass matrix, simple facet and edge-based nGL and GLSL, and many core functions for matrix Eigen feel a lot more like MATLAB.
